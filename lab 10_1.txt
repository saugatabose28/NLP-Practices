Pinecone

Go to http://app.pinecone.io/ and create an account



Complete your information, selecting Python





Scroll down and press the eye to reveal your API key



Write down the key.

OpenAI

Go to https://platform.openai.com/overview and select sign up in the top right



Create an account and verify it by clicking the link in the email you receive.

Hover over the left side menu and select "API keys"



Verify your mobile phone number.

Select 'Create new secret key'

Enter any name and select any project (e.g., "COMP5046 key" and "Default project"). Set permissions to 'all' and press 'Create secret key'

Copy the key (it will not be accessible once you click 'Done' or close this window).
